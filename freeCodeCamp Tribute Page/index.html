Siouxsie Sioux

Susan Janet Ballion, known professionally as Siouxsie Sioux, is an English singer, songwriter, musician, and record producer. She is best known as the lead singer of the rock band Siouxsie and the Banshees. (Wikipedia)
Born: May 27, 1957 · Chislehurst, Kent, England, UK
Birth name: Susan Janet Ballion
Nicknames: The Godmother of Goth, The High Priestess of Punk, The Ice Queen, The Goth-Pop Priestess, Punk's Princess, First Lady of Punk, The Original "Punk Vamp", The Priestess of Post-Punk
Height: 5′ 8″ (1.73 m)
